Welcome to Infinity Lab!

Infinity Lab is a student-run organization bringing music, creativity, and joy to seniors while providing mentorship and learning opportunities to children in underserved communities.

Organization Information

Organization Name: Infinity Lab

Type: California Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation

Mission & Objectives

Infinity Lab empowers musically talented and tech-savvy youth to serve the community. Through music performances, interactive events, technology outreach, and educational programs, we help students develop leadership while uplifting community well-being.

Primary Activities

Nursing Home Services: Youth volunteers perform music and host interactive games for seniors.

Children’s Education at Libraries: We partner with libraries to provide free programs in music, art, and STEAM for children in underserved areas.

Infinity Lab is a nonprofit public benefit group registered in California. We do not hold 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status. All volunteer hours are tracked and certification is available upon request.
