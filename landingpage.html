Welcome to Infinity Lab!

Infinity Lab is a student-run organization bringing music and joy to seniors and providing learning opportunities to children in communities that need them.

Organization Information

Organization Name: Infinity Lab

Type: Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation (California)

Mission and Objectives

Infinity Lab is committed to empowering musically talented and tech-savvy youth to serve the community. Through music performances, gaming activities, technology outreach to seniors, and mentorship for children, we foster leadership and communication skills while promoting community well-being.

Primary Activities

Nursing Home Services: We recruit youth volunteers to perform music and organize interactive games to enhance seniors' well-being.

Children’s Education at Libraries: We collaborate with libraries to offer free educational programs in music, art, and STEAM for children in under-resourced areas.

Infinity Lab is a California-based nonprofit public benefit organization. It has not applied for 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status. Volunteer hours are tracked and recorded after each service, and records can be accessed by members at any time.
