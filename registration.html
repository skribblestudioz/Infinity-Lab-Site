Join Us

Our team needs student volunteers!
Jobs: Performing music to seniors, & hosting board games for seniors.
You are expected to commit 1-3 hours a month on Sunday afternoons. We warmly welcome students with artistic talent and a passion for performance to join us at any time. (Priority: Violinists, flutists, clarinet, harpists, guitarists, saxophonists, singers, and dancers)
If you are interested in becoming a performer, fill out this form.
If you are interested in hosting board games, fill out this form.
Or contact us at infinitylab2024@gmail.com

About Service Hours:
Our organization is a nonprofit volunteer group (Nonprofit Corporation - CA - Public Benefit), but does not have 501(c)(3) status. We record service hours for all volunteers and provide certification upon request. Recognition of volunteer hours completed with us depends on acceptance by other organizations.
